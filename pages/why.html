Scratching Our Own Itch

We sought feedback from design friends through various channels like email, Dropbox, Droplr, Skype, and Facebook. However, the feedback was scattered, lost, and unclear. In search of a solution, we explored numerous design feedback apps. However, they were all overly complex and distracting, with excessive features and claims to solve every problem. We craved simplicity, so we created our own solution.

What Motivate Us

We want to make feedback an egoless standard; an indispensable part of the design process. We want to do our part to encourage designers to seek feedback and not praise. To enable designers to truly collaborate with every discipline that make up a team.

Not A Project Management Tool

We specialize in visual collaboration and documentation, prioritizing effective message delivery. Our simplicity sets us apart from complex tools that aim to do it all. While we're not a project management tool, we seamlessly integrate with popular platforms like Basecamp, Slack, Jira, and Trello. Just add a FeedBerry link to your existing communication method for a seamless experience.


User Experience Over Features

We aim for a seamless feedback experience that feels natural. Speed is crucial to keep up with your thoughts. Any feature we introduce serves the cohesive experience and enhances the user's life. It exists solely for these purposes.
